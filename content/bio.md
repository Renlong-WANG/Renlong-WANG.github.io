I recently completed my M.S. in Industrial Engineering and Management at the [University of Chinese Academy of Sciences](https://english.ucas.ac.cn/index.php/about-ucas/introduction) (UCAS), advised by Dr. [Mingang Gao](https://people.ucas.ac.cn/~0028787) at the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). I received my B.Eng. in Civil Engineering from [Nanjing Tech University](https://en.njtech.edu.cn/About/General_Information.htm) in 2023, advised by Dr. [Lingzhi Li](https://cce.njtech.edu.cn/info/1098/3647.htm).

My research develops optimization, simulation, and data-driven methods for complex systems under uncertainty, with a particular focus on infrastructure resilience, emergency management, and robust decision-making. I am especially interested in how infrastructure systems can be modeled, compared, and restored under uncertain hazards, limited resources, and heterogeneous stakeholder preferences.
